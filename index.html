About the Challenge

Mosaic 2025 is a unique short film challenge where participants have exactly 96 hours to create a short film from scratch. Test your creativity, storytelling skills, and filmmaking abilities in this exciting competition!

About Mosaic 2025

Mosaic 2025 is a 96-hour short film challenge. This unique event challenges filmmakers to create a short film within just 4 days, pushing their creative boundaries and technical skills to the limit.

The challenge is open to all students. Teams will have to showcase their storytelling abilities, technical expertise, and teamwork within the given time frame.
