Contest Rules & Guidelines

  • THE SHORT FILM SHOULD BE OF UPTO MAXIMUM 20 MINUTES OF LENGTH AND MINIMUM OF 8 MINUTES, INCLUDING THE CREDITS.
  • ALL THE SHORT FILMS CAN BE OF ANY LANGUAGE BUT MUST BE SUBTITLED IN ENGLISH.
  • THE COLLEGE RESERVES THE RIGHTS TO PUBLISH THE SHORTLISTED CONTENT IN ITS OFFICIAL SOCIAL MEDIA CHANNELS WITH SUITABLE CREDITS.
  • OUT OF ALL SUBMISSIONS, ONLY THE BEST FEW ARE SELECTED FOR SCREENING ON 15TH APRIL 2025.
  • REGISTRATION FEE OF 169/- PER TEAM IS MANDATORY AND IS NO-REFUNDABLE. THIS REGISTRATION FEE IS ONLY FOR THE SHORT FILM CONTEST.
  • NO OBSCENITY IN ANY FORM OF AMBIGUOUS OR PROVOCATIVE DIALOGUE AND ATTIRE WILL BE TOLERATED.
  • SUBMISSIONS CONTAINING COPYRIGHTED MATERIAL OR PLAGIARIZED CONTENT WILL BE EXCLUDED FROM CONSIDERATION.
  • LAST DATE FOR SUBMISSION OF SHORT FILM IS 10TH APRIL 2025, 10AM.
  • THE ORGANISING COMMITTEE HOLDS THE ULTIMATE AUTHORITY ON ALL DECISIONS.
